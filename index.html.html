About Me

Born and raised in southwest Denver. I met my wife while attending CSU in Fort Collins. We have been married 5 years and have been together for over 12 years. We have a dog named Diamond and we currently live in Thornton, CO.

I have a BA in Economics from Colorado State University. I currently work for Jefferson County Public Library. I have worked in the Public Procurement sector since graduating. I am new to coding and the computer science realm. I may be out of my element right now, but by the end of this bootcamp, my goal will be to have the tools and knowledge to be successful and develop web content to the best of my abilities. I am ready for a career change out of government procurement. I am all in on this coding stuff!

I enjoy spending time with my family, traveling and going to concerts - particularly Red Rocks & The Gothic. I also like to root for our local sports teams - Go Broncos, Rox, Nuggets, Avs, Rapids, Outlaws, Mammoth, Rams and even the Buffs (when they aren't playing the Rams)!
