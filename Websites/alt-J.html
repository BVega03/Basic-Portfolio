alt-J Website

alt-J is one of the smartest, musically gifted and innovative bands of all time and they are only getting started. The English indie rock band formed in 2008. alt-J is named for the keys entered on a Mac keyboard, which turns out to be the delta symbol. They have a folky-electronic and alternative rock sound with one of a kind vocals. Easily one of my favorite bands out today and alt-J is definitely making it's way to the top with incredible live performances to add to the table.

They are a great follow on social media facebook.com instagram.com twitter.com
